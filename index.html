情境一：機上生存
On the Plane · Today's Focus
今日完成度
💬 萬用句型Universal Patterns — 點擊展開例句
1
I'll have the [chicken / beef], please.點機上餐 — 填入你的選擇
▼
Vegetarian: Do you have a vegetarian option?
拒絕: No thank you, I'll skip the meal.
進階: Is there any MSG / peanuts in this?
2
Can I have a [Coke], no ice, please?飲料去冰 — 今天已掌握 ✅
▼
熱飲: Can I have a hot tea / coffee, please?
加量: Could I have some more water?
泰文提示: ไม่ใส่น้ำแข็ง (mai sai nam khaeng) = 去冰
3
Could I borrow a [blanket / pen]?借用物品 — 今天已掌握 ✅
▼
枕頭: Could I have an extra pillow?
眼罩: Do you have an eye mask / earplugs?
入境卡: Could I have an arrival card for Thailand?
4
Where is the lavatory / restroom?找廁所 — 基礎必備
▼
飛機上通常說 lavatory 或 restroom
占用燈: Occupied = 有人，Vacant = 沒人

情境三：泰國網卡辦理完整攻略
Thailand SIM Card Guide · BKK Airport
準備度
📍

蘇萬納普機場 (BKK)：入境大廳有 AIS、DTAC、TrueMove H 三家，建議選 AIS Tourist SIM（訊號最穩）。要求店員當場設定 APN 並測試網速。

🎭 情境對話
🏪 Staff
Hello! Which plan would you like?
✈ You
Hi! I'd like a tourist SIM card, please. Which plan is best for 10 days?
🏪 Staff
This one is 299 baht — unlimited data for 15 days.
✈ You
That sounds good. Does it include a hotspot?
🏪 Staff
Can I see your passport?
✈ You
Sure, here it is. Could you help me insert the SIM and make sure it's working?
🏪 Staff
Of course! What's your phone model?
✈ You
It's an iPhone [15]. And could you show me how to set the APN?

情境五：機上突發狀況（進階練習）
Emergency on Board · Advanced Challenge
難度
🎯

這是今天的進階挑戰！以下是你沒有練習過的機上突發情境。試著自己先想想怎麼說，再展開答案。

💬 突發情境Advanced — 先想再看答案
A
你感覺身體不舒服，想請空服員幫忙🤔 你會怎麼說？
▼
標準句： Excuse me, I'm not feeling well. Could I get some help?
頭暈： I feel dizzy and nauseous. Do you have any medication?
緊急： I need a doctor. Is there a doctor on board?
B
你不小心把飲料打翻在座位上🤔 你會怎麼說？
▼
標準句： I'm so sorry — I spilled my drink. Could I have some napkins / paper towels?
追加： Could I also change my seat? My seat is all wet.
C
旁邊的乘客一直打呼吵到你睡覺🤔 你會怎麼說（問空服員）？
▼
標準句： Excuse me, is there any other available seat? My neighbor is snoring quite loudly.
委婉版： Could I possibly move to a different seat?
備用： Do you have any earplugs?
D
你的行李被放進別人的行李廂，空間不夠🤔 你會怎麼說？
▼
標準句： Excuse me, there's no space in the overhead bin. Could you help me find a spot?
or： Could you check if this bag can be stored somewhere else?
